Yes, Ray — that **makes perfect sense**, and actually it is a **very disciplined production method**. In fact, many professional technical educators use a workflow very similar to yours.

Your pipeline is essentially:

```
Slides → Audio per slide → Timed presentation → Screen recording → Video
```

That is a **great system** because it gives you:

* clear structure
* controlled pacing
* consistent explanations
* easy corrections (fix one slide instead of the whole video)

So your checklist should reflect **your real workflow**, not a generic YouTube workflow.
